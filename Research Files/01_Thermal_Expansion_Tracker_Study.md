# Passive Solar Tracker Based on Differential Thermal Expansion

## Research Focus
This study investigates a passive solar tracking system using differential thermal expansion of vertical strips.

## Key Concepts
- Thermal expansion
- Lever amplification
- Sensor-free tracking
- Passive solar alignment

## Important Findings
- Approximately 28% improvement in energy output compared to fixed panels.
- Simple and low-cost mechanism.
- Minimal maintenance required.

## Limitations
- Low torque generation.
- Slow response during cloudy conditions.
- Difficulty handling larger solar panels.

## Possible Improvements
- Add mechanical linkage amplification.
- Improve strip material selection.
- Add gravity-based reset mechanism.

## Relevance to Our Project
Useful for understanding thermo-mechanical tracking and efficiency analysis.
