# Fluid-Based Passive Solar Tracking System

## Research Focus
This paper studies passive solar tracking using fluid expansion mechanisms.

## Key Concepts
- Fluid thermal expansion
- Weight imbalance tracking
- Dual-container balancing system

## Important Findings
- Smooth tracking movement.
- Better torque generation than thermal strip systems.
- Improved efficiency over fixed systems.

## Limitations
- Fluid leakage risk.
- Temperature dependency.
- Slow response at low sunlight intensity.

## Possible Improvements
- Better sealing methods.
- Optimized fluid selection.
- Improved balancing arm design.

## Relevance to Our Project
Provides the core idea for developing a low-cost passive solar tracker.
