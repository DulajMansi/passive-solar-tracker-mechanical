# Review of Passive Solar Tracking Systems

## Research Focus
Comparative review of different passive solar tracking methods.

## Tracking Types
- Thermal expansion systems
- Fluid expansion systems
- Gravity-based systems
- Shape-memory systems

## Important Findings
- Passive systems reduce cost and maintenance.
- Active systems are more accurate.
- Passive systems are suitable for small-scale applications.

## Major Problems Identified
- Low tracking precision.
- Weather dependency.
- No night reset mechanism.
- Wind instability.

## Opportunities for Innovation
- Add damping systems.
- Develop reset mechanisms.
- Improve balancing structures.

## Relevance to Our Project
Useful for literature review and identifying research gaps.
