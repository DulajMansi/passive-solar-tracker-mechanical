# Night Reset Mechanism for Passive Solar Trackers

## Research Focus
Study of automatic reset mechanisms for passive solar trackers.

## Problem Addressed
Passive trackers often fail to return to the starting position after sunset.

## Existing Solutions
- Spring return systems
- Gravity-assisted reset
- Small motor-assisted reset

## Important Findings
- Gravity reset systems are low-cost.
- Spring systems require calibration.
- Reset improves morning efficiency.

## Proposed Improvement
Develop a purely mechanical gravity reset system.

## Relevance to Our Project
Provides a possible innovation area for improving passive tracking systems.
