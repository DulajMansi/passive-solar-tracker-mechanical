# Dual-Axis Passive Solar Tracking Analysis

## Research Focus
Analysis of dual-axis passive solar tracking systems.

## Key Concepts
- East-West rotation
- North-South tilt adjustment
- Dual-axis balancing

## Important Findings
- Dual-axis systems improve solar capture.
- More complex than single-axis systems.
- Better efficiency during seasonal changes.

## Challenges
- Increased structural complexity.
- Stability under wind loading.
- Higher balancing requirements.

## Possible Improvements
- Lightweight frame design.
- Better pivot mechanisms.
- Improved balancing methods.

## Relevance to Our Project
Helpful for understanding advanced passive solar tracking mechanisms.
